Kia Picanto 2023

Now on its 3rd-generation model, the Kia Picanto still bears the same youthful and energetic qualities, yet more cabin space due to the 15 mm increase in its wheelbase. What changed though was the improvement in balance and responsive driving experience, thanks to the chassis upgrade.
